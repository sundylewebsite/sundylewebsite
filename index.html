About Us

A Fort Wayne, Indiana based 6-piece ensemble that radiates the warmth of jazz, the enchanting vibes of neo-soul, and the rhythmic heartbeats of R&B. Hailing from diverse musical backgrounds, the members of SUN.DYLE converge to create a harmonious blend of genres that persuades the listener to feel, think and transcend.




Faintest Idea

SUN.DYLE's latest EP, "Faintest Idea," casts a mesmerizing spell, weaving intricate rhythms and soulful melodies that beckon listeners into a realm of neo-soul jazz fusion. The band's masterful instrumentation creates a rich tapestry of sound, where velvety vocals intertwine with hypnotic basslines and stunning guitar textures, evoking a sense of both introspection and celebration. "Faintest Idea" showcases SUN.DYLE's adeptness at crafting music that transcends genres, inviting audiences to explore the depths of emotion and groove in a harmonious fusion that leaves an indelible mark on the soul.
